The United States Of America 

 

In the United States, there are a few distinct traditions typically celebrated each year. Some of the most common traditions are:

  • Christmas trees
  • Gift-giving
  • Santa (tecnically not a tradition but oh well)

Christmas Trees

In the united states, one of the most common traditions is the Christmas tree. Some people use real trees while others use artificial ones. Often times people will decorate these trees with ornaments and lights, with a star (or other ornament) on top. Gifts are usually placed underneath the tree, leading me to the next tradition:

Gift Giving

In the U.S., gift giving is a common tradition, with about 69% of Americans participating in a gift exchange each year. (Yes, this is a real statistic.) Typically, gifts are wrapped and placed under the Christmas tree. On Christmas day, people then open the presents.

Santa

The "mascot" of Christmas in the United States, Santa goes by many names. Santa Clause, Saint Nick, and more.
